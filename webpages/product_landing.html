Fitness Services

Sometimes, you need a little help to achieve your fitness goals. Our certified personal training staff can help tailor an exercise program specific to you. We have services that will provide detailed instruction, personal accountability, and motivation. Exercise programming, equipment orientations, and private group classes and workshops are available to you at no or minimal cost.

Fitness Equipment Orientation

This 60 minute orientation will teach users how to use cardio, strength, and flexibility equipment in our facility safely and effectively. This service is perfect for new members or members who have questions about our equipment.

Personal Training

All of our personal training sessions are one hour in length. Our personal trainers are certified through one of the top national certification agencies such as ACSM, NSCA, ACE or NASM. Many of our trainers also have a degree in the field of Exercise Science or are currently pursuing their degree in such a field.
