St Vincent and the Grenadines

Caribbean sea view from Kingstown, St Vincent
The Land of the Blessed

St Vincent and the Grenadines is a country found in the Caribbean sea. It is a part of the Lesser Antilles, with St Lucia to its north, Barbados to east and Grenada to its south.

The country encompasses 32 islands surrounded by beautiful turquoise water. St Vincent (the main land) is north of the Grenadine islands which span south towards Grenada.

The country's beaches are one of the most breathtaking and diverse in the Caribbean sea. St Vincent comprises of both black sand and white sand beaches. The black sand beaches originates from its active volcano La Soufriére.

Warm weather, slow-pace, and local festivals, makes this country an ideal vacation destination.
